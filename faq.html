Notice: We have recently been notified that someone is misusing our name and pretending to be RedCarpet. If you are contacted by a person that asks for money to complete a loan transaction and claims to be from RedCarpet, it is a scam. RedCarpet does not ask for money in order to complete loan applications. RedCarpet does not require you to send money in order to obtain a loan. If you are are contacted by anyone representing to be RedCarpet and you are unsure whether it is RedCarpet, please do not provide any information or money.
