100% dedicated to our customers.

Very simple - RedCarpet lends to customers in India to finance their online purchases - e-commerce, travel, etc. In India, credit and finance companies are able to service less than 3% of the customer base because there is no data, no widespread credit bureaus to profile and score customers. RedCarpet has access to much more data, including data on your mobile phone. We use this data to service customers who never had any access to credit before.

We NEVER give out cash - we complete the purchase for you on other e-commerce sites. So our lending is very closely tied to shopping (rather than pay for your school fees, etc.). Remember in India, there are no credit bureaus... no real way of figuring out risk of people. So our secret sauce is this data science that figures out people's risk.

Successful borrowers see their rates go down over time.
